FreeTube is a privacy-focused YouTube client that allows you to enjoy your favorite content without your viewing habits being tracked. It offers an ad-free experience, offline viewing capabilities, and a familiar YouTube-like interface. Your viewing history and subscriptions are stored locally, ensuring your data remains private. FreeTube is free and open-source software, promoting transparency and community involvement.
