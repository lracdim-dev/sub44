Uptime Kuma is a free and open-source monitoring tool designed to help you keep track of the availability and performance of your websites, servers, and applications. It provides a simple and intuitive interface that allows you to easily monitor the status of your services and receive notifications when issues arise. With Uptime Kuma, you can rest assured that your critical systems are always up and running.
