Openpilot is an open-source self-driving car project that aims to make advanced driver assistance systems (ADAS) accessible to a wider range of vehicles. By using a combination of hardware and software, openpilot can provide features like automatic steering, adaptive cruise control, and lane keeping assistance. It's a community-driven project, meaning anyone can contribute to its development and improvement.
