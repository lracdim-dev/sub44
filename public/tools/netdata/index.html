Netdata is a real-time performance monitoring tool that provides detailed insights into your system's health and resource usage. It collects and visualizes metrics from various sources, including CPU, memory, disk, network, and processes. Netdata's user-friendly interface allows you to easily identify performance bottlenecks and troubleshoot issues. With its lightweight design and low overhead, Netdata is suitable for monitoring systems of all sizes.
